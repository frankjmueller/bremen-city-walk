Bremen City Walk

Your offline pocket guide

frankjmueller.github.io/bremen-city-walk

  1. Scan the code at breakfast (hotel Wi-Fi).
  2. Wait for the green “Offline ready” badge — takes a few seconds.
  3. Walk! Flight mode is fine — pictures, maps buttons and audio stories all work offline.

Tram 6 → Cathedral → Town Musicians → Roland → the Bremen Hole → Böttcherstraße → Schnoor → Schlachte (lunch!) → the Pigs → Windmill → the Elephant & Namibia memorial → Tram 6 home.
Tip: download the offline map of Bremen in Google Maps before you go.
